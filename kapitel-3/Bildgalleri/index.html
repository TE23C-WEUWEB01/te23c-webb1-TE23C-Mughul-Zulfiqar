Saturn Apollo Program

The second manned lunar landing mission, Apollo 12
launched from launch pad 39-A at Kennedy Space Center
in Florida on November 14, 1969 via a Saturn Five launch
vehicle. The Saturn V vehicle was developed by the
Marshall Space Flight Center (MSFC) under the direction
of Dr. Wernher von Braun.
